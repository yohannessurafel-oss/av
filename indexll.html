📖 AVMF Core Banking System — User Manual

Standard Operating Guide for Ledger Control & Credit Operations

1. Core Architecture Overview

The AVMF CBS v2.0 Platform functions as a centralized ledger engine interfacing operational data models directly with high-integrity database schemas (Supabase backend synchronization). Operational tasks must follow a rigorous registration-appraisal-sanction sequence to preserve legal credit parameters.

2. Workflow Lifecycle & Procedures

A. Client Management
  • Master Registry: Input full verification data, KYC parameters, and record unique identifying details.
  • Account Maintenance: Allocate secondary saving pockets, current sub-ledgers, or dedicated loan holding routes.
B. Credit Processing Cycle
  • Application Setup: Register individualized or structured sub-group loan requests.
  • Risk Appraisal: Process debt-service ratios (DSR) and collateral asset valuations.
  • Sanction Controls: Authorize official credit allocations via approval checkpoints.
C. Ledger & Teller Controls
  • Vault Management: Tellers must declare structural coin/note breakdowns at open and close shifts.
  • Atomic Allocation: System automatically funnels repayment capital down sequentially into accrued Penalties → Due Interest → Core Principal.

3. System Support & Exception Resolution

For transaction rollback requests, structural schema mismatches, or diagnostic checking overrides, navigate to the Diagnostics & Tools category to run the live Supabase Test Harness, or request infrastructure clearance from your Lead IT Systems Administrator.
